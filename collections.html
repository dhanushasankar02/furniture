The Modern Biophilic Series

Collection 02

The Mid-Century Classic

Wall-spanning storage consoles built from rich American Walnut. Features brass hardware detailing, offset book storage shelves, and vintage leather playback armchairs. Each unit is individually numbered and hand-polished to enhance the natural grain of the wood, bringing a warm, nostalgic acoustic-lounge feel to modern high-fidelity listening spaces.

Timber Materials Solid American Walnut
Hardware Details Solid Hand-Finished Brass
Design Origin Copenhagen, Denmark
Acoustic Tuning Resonance Dampening
