Timeline

From Blueprint To Reality

Stage 01

Bespoke Design & Dimensions

Submit your measurements, select wood species and fabrics. Our spatial architects build a custom rendering matching your room.

Stage 02

Timber Selection

Our carpenters hand-pick slabs of timber, selecting the most structural grain lines and checking wood moisture density levels.

Stage 03

Precision Joinery & Crafting

Mortise and tenon joints are hand-slotted and joined without metal screws, preserving timber structure and classic look.

Stage 04

Finishing & Delivery

Each piece is hand-rubbed with natural oil wax, dried, and shipped in white-glove packaging directly to your room.
