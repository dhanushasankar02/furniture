Furniture Care

Preserving the Organic Value

LIGNUM designer creations are protected by natural organic oil treatments. With simple care guidelines, the warm textures and structural stability of your custom woods will endure for generations.

1

Hydration & Wax Treatment

Apply premium natural wood wax oil once every six months to keep the oak fibers elastic and prevent dry splitting.

2

Sunlight & Humidity Balance

Position your solid wood items away from direct vents and intense windows to maintain uniform internal moisture content.
